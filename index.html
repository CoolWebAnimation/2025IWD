Ode to a Diva



You are a spotlight-stealing star,
Whose laughter echoes near and far.
A whirlwind of wit, a fountain of fun,
The ultimate diva when all's said and done. Your jokes like diamonds, sparkling bright,
Your presence turns day from darkest night.
With sass unbounded and style divine,
The world's your stage and the spotlight's thine. A melody of mirth, a chorus of glee,
You bring such joy for all to see.
Your dramatic entrance stops the show,
The greatest diva the world will know. Fabulous and fearless, shining so bright,
A comedic genius, pure delight.
The world's best diva without compare,
Magnificent in jest beyond what's fair.
Happy International Women Day
to the world's best Diva!
